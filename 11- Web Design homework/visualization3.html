Lattitude vs. Cloudiness

Cloudiness and latitude do not show a correlation in the five hundred data points viewed. Significant variability is shown in all latitudes in relation to average cloudiness. Position on Earth does not affect cloudiness. Further analysis between cloudiness and maximum temperature is recommended to view a possible correlation.
